## Key Visual Principles

From El Lissitzky, Rodchenko, and the Stenberg Brothers:

1. **Diagonal composition** — Dynamic angles, 15-45° tilts
2. **Geometric shapes** — Triangles, circles, bold rectangles
3. **Minimal color** — Red/black/white dominates, gold accent
4. **Bold typography** — Condensed, sans-serif, often tilted
5. **Photomontage elements** — Cut-out imagery layered with geometry
6. **Asymmetric balance** — Tension through placement
7. **Industrial feel** — Art as engineering, not decoration
